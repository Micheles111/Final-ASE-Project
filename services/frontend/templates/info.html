How to Play "La Escoba"

The goal is to capture cards from the table by matching sums of 15.

  • The Deck: 40 Spanish cards (Oros, Copas, Espadas, Bastos).
  • Capture Rule: Play a card from your hand. If your card + one or more cards on the table sum exactly to 15, you capture them all!
  • Values:
    • Cards 1-7: Face value.
    • Sota (10): Worth 8.
    • Caballo (11): Worth 9.
    • Rey (12): Worth 10.
  • Escoba: If you clear the entire table, you score 1 point immediately (Escoba!).
  • End Game: When the deck is empty, the last player who captured cards takes everything remaining on the table.

Scoring Points

  • Escobas: 1 point each.
  • Most Cards: 1 point for > 20 cards.
  • Most Coins (Oros): 1 point for > 5 coins.
  • Settebello (7 of Coins): 1 point.
